Custom Homes

For more than two decades, Ryner Homes based in Snohomish, Washington, has built custom homes for people throughout the area. Trust our custom home professionals to give you the home of your dreams.

Building Homes

Ryner Homes has been building northwest custom homes for more than 20 years in Snohomish and North King Counties. We've built and mastered our approach in the process.

Count on Us

Count on us to stay on schedule, while adhering to the best practices developed and proven over time. Also count on us to be around for decades to come, and to stand by the reputation we've carefully built.

What to Expect

Timeless artistry and meticulous workmanship, exceptional attention to detail and proportion, distinctive mill work and luxurious amenities—these are the qualities you've come to expect from Ryner Homes time and again. Ryner Homes’ homeowners tell us that the major attribute that drew them to our company is the integrity of the experience.

We design and build every home to offer a seamless union of tradition, character, and sophistication. From our use of top-of-the-line materials to the quality of craftsmanship that goes into every detail and every room, this is the home that will continue to reward you in comfort and style.

Your Ryner Homes home is also an investment that will pay you back as you enjoy living in a spacious, luxurious home now—and again if you resell it later.

Energy-Efficient/Green

At Ryner Homes, we believe in being more than just the next green builder; we believe in going above and beyond the norm to build the most energy-efficient homes in the country. Some home builders take a step in the right direction, but stop there and call themselves green. We, on the other hand, decided to build custom homes differently, from the ground up.

Building green means getting more performance from your home or building with less environmental impact compared to traditional construction. You can build a healthy and efficient home to be proud of.
